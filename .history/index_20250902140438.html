ABOUT ME

Hey, I’m Alec Garnica — a mixing engineer, producer, and audio nerd who’s been at it for the past 13 years. Over the years I’ve had the chance to work with some amazing artists like American Arson, Philip Bowen, and the Scally Brothers, as well as with Facedown Records and even on film trailers like Eternal Theater.

I mostly work in the world of rock, pop, punk, and pop-rock, and I love crafting mixes that are polished, modern, and full of energy. My style has been shaped by learning under some legendary names in the industry like Chris Lord-Alge, Dave Hagen, and Tim Padalin — so I put the same level of care and punch into every project I take on.

When I mix, my goal is simple: bring your song to life. I want it to have feel and soul, while still sounding massive and ready to compete in today’s music landscape.

I work out of my studio just outside of Detroit, but thanks to the beauty of the internet, I collaborate with artists all over the world. Besides mixing, I also offer editing, backtracking synth production, and re-amping to help you get your songs exactly where you want them.

At the end of the day, I’m here to make your music shine. Let’s make something incredible together.
